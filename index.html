Studio philosophy

Sewing as a calm, everyday ritual

Blueberry Patterns is a small digital pattern studio built around quiet mornings, long afternoons at the cutting table, and the feeling of wearing something you made with your own hands.

It’s for sewists who love clean lines, cloud-like palettes, and a sense of gentle luxury — without rushing, without overcomplication.
